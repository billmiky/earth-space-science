Earth‚Äôs temperature is rising, reversing the cooling trend that occurred over the past 2,000 years, putting millions of people in harm‚Äôs way, yet the public is not convinced of the risks.

  • What are 99.9% of climate scientists seeing in historical climate data that makes them so sure humans are the cause of climate change today?
  • Why does it matter?
