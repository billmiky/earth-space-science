The Bottom Line: - ‚úÖ Climate change is definitely caused by human activity - ‚úÖ Natural factors cannot explain current warming - ‚úÖ Increasing temperatures will affect sea ice, sea level, weather patterns, food, and water - ‚úÖ Climate change will affect billions through disasters, displacement, and resource shortages - ‚úÖ Understanding the past helps us prepare for and potentially prevent future disasters
